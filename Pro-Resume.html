Resume Writing Tips

Keep It Concise

Limit your resume to 1-2 pages. Recruiters typically spend only a few seconds scanning each resume.

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "Developed", "Led", "Implemented" to showcase your impact.

Quantify Achievements

Where possible, include numbers to demonstrate your accomplishments (e.g., "Increased sales by 30%").

Tailor for Each Job

Customize your resume for each position by highlighting relevant skills and experiences.
